Mission

An AI operating system for certifiable aerospace autonomy.

D³ARWIN is a research program at Texas A&M University developing the core AI architecture for autonomous aerospace systems that cannot afford uncertain decisions. The program spans uncertainty quantification, real-time data assimilation, physics-informed surrogates, and policy optimization, unified into a closed-loop decision architecture that is auditable, certifiable, and deployable at the edge.
